Sandborne

Sandborne was my team senior capstone project for the 3D Game Development Capstone at UT Austin. I worked with a six person team where I served as the Tools and AI Programmer and Release Engineer. Sandborne allowed me to develop my skills in Unity, Graphics, AI, and Tools Development.
We completed Sandborne in one semester, roughly three months, and shipped to itch.io with Windows, MacOS, and Web builds. Below are parts of Sandborne that I worked on. You can also play for yourself!

My Contributions

Behavior Tree Tool

The Unity Behavior Tree Tool is a full behavior tree implementation built in Unity complete with a in-editor GUI built with Unity's UI Toolkit and GraphView. My main motivation to make this was to aid in the development of Sandborne. It was instrumental in us implementing the two multi-staged boss fights, Screwman and Batteryman in the tight three-month timeline that we were working with.
